Made with juicy Italian tomatoes

Product Detail:

The star of the Stokes range! 200g of sun-ripened, juicy Italian tomatoes are crammed into every 100g of this award-winning ketchup, giving it a rich tomatoey flavour which makes you wonder how you hadn’t discovered it before! Perfect with fish and chips, bangers & mash, and the great British breakfast. Also a fantastic ingredient for your favourite recipes too like Shepherds Pie or Spaghetti Bolognese. Available in our regular 300g bottle or the larger 580g bottle for true Ketchup fans! The Great Taste award judges commented ‘A very good textured ketchup. It’s smooth and glossy. There is definitely a lot of quality tomato in here.’


ingredients

Tomatoes (200g in 100g ketchup), Unrefined Raw Cane Sugar, Spirit Vinegar, Cornflour, Sea Salt, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Seasoning (Salt, Spice Extracts (contains Celery), Onion Extract), Sorbic Acid.
